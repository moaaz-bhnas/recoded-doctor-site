Clinical Psychologist registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa.

I am a Clinical Psychologist in private practice based in Pietermaritzburg. Below you will find information regarding the types of services that I offer as well as some general information regarding psychotherapy.

I can help you with a wide range of psychological problems and offer therapy to individuals, couples and groups.

Training

Before training to be a Clinical Psychologist, I completed a Master’s degree in philosophy and lectured philosophy to both undergraduates and postgraduates. It was my interest in Philosophy of Mind, lead me to studying psychology.

About

I am a clinical psychologist registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa. I have my Private Practice in Pietermaritzburg. I completed my Clinical Psychology internship at the Fort Napier/ Townhill Complex in Pietermaritzburg and completed my Community service at Greys Hospital (also In Pietermaritzburg).

Specialities

I have a special interest in the treatment of Anxiety and depression as well as the management of ADHD symptoms. As well as the treatment of anxiety, depression and relational problems which so often come with ADHD.

Services

I provide psychotherapy for individuals, couples, groups from my practice in Pietermaritzburg. I also offer online psychotherapy for those who are unable to make it to my office. I will also take on parents who require assistance in the management of ADHD children of any age.

As a Clinical Psychologist I provide psychological treatment for a wide range of mental health difficulties, some of which are listed below.

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Life changes
  • Relationship difficulties
  • ADHD Management for adults and children
  • Bereavement
  • Coping with medical illness
  • Addiction

I will travel to general hospital where necessary
